    Studio Grain is a boutique design studio based in Bangalore, India. Grain is a metaphor for detail, tiny particle, life and just growth in general. Smaller details put together are instrumental in creating the bigger picture, which is where Studio Grain comes in.

    Thoughtful and strong visuals go a long way and are the key ingredients to communicating ideas and thoughts. We believe in creating designs that are impactful, engaging and aesthetically appealing.
    Founder

    Priyanka Ranka is an illustrator and graphic designer from Bangalore. She graduated with a diploma in illustration from Srishti School of Design & Technology. Though she started out as an illustrator, visual communication is something she picked up on the job and now both of them go hand in hand.

    Nature and nostalgia are imbibed in her work. She takes inspiration from the mundane surroundings mixing it up with surreal thoughts. On an idle day, you would find her illustrating something botanical and filling it up with stippled details.
